## WPOven Free Email 

### Introduction
WPOven allows you to create full-featured mailboxes using your own domain name directly from your dashboard. This guide will walk you through the precise steps to provision a new mailbox.

<!-- <span style="{{ 'color:red' }}">Each account comes with 1 free email , additional emails cost 1 USD / month. </span> -->
:::warning Pricing

Each account includes **1 free email mailbox**.

Additional mailboxes are billed at **$1/month per mailbox**.

:::

### Prerequisites
* Your domain must be active and correctly pointed to your WPOven nameservers or server IP address.
* You will need DNS access to update DNS records. 


### Step-by-Step Account Creation
1. **Navigate to the Emails Section:** Log in to your WPOven Dashboard and click on the **Emails** tab located in the top main navigation menu.
2. **Access the Creation Form:** Click the **Add Email** button to open the configuration interface (as shown in `image_7aee6c.png`).
3. **Configure the Email Fields:**
   * **Email Type:** Leave this set to the default **Normal** option. 
   * **Name:** Input the user's **First Name** and **Last Name** into the respective text fields.
   * **Email Account:** Enter your desired email prefix (e.g., `info` or `support`) in the first box, then select your target domain from the dropdown menu to the right of the `@` symbol.
   * **Email password:** Enter a strong, secure password for the mailbox.
4. **Deploy the Mailbox:** Click the green **Add Email** button at the bottom of the form to finalize deployment and allocate the address.

### Post-Creation Management
* **Autorization** Once Email is created you will need to update your DNS records with provided records for authorization to Enable it. 
* **Changing Passwords:** If you ever need to reset a user password, locate the email address within the **Emails** tab, click **Edit**, and update the password field.
* **Deleting an Account:** To permanently delete a mailbox, click the Delete Action for the specific email. *Warning: This action deletes all stored emails on the server permanently.*
